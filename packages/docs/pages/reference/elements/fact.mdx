# `<fact>`

The `<fact>` tag creates a container for the statement of a fact with optional
`title`, `creator`, and `proof`. The structure of the `fact` tag is completely
analogous to that of the following tags:
[`algorithm`](./algorithm), [`claim`](./claim), [`corollary`](./corollary),
[`identity`](./identity), [`lemma`](./lemma), [`proposition`](./proposition),
and [`theorem`](./theorem).

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="p">
<fact xml:id="fact-law-of-cosines">
  <title>Law of Cosines</title>
  <statement>
    <p>
      Given any triangle with two sides having lengths <m>a</m>, <m>b</m>
      and angle <m>\theta</m> between them,
      the length, <m>c</m>, of the third side is given by:
      <me>
        c^2 = a^2 + b^2 -2ab\cos\theta.
      </me>
    </p>
  </statement>
</fact>
</FRAGMENT>
```
