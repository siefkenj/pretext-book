# `<acknowledgement>`

The `<acknowledgement>` tag is an optional element of [`<frontmatter>`](./frontmatter).  It 
becomes its own division and can contain paragraphs, lists, etc. It is generally used for text
thanking people who made the project possible.  Related to, but distinct from, [`<dedication>`](./dedication) 
 and [`<contributors>`](./contributors).

## Syntax

<SyntaxDisplay name="acknowledgement" />

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="frontmatter">
    <acknowledgement>
        <p>
            I am grateful to all the people who read this and made suggestions or otherwise
        made this possible.
            <ul>
                <li>
                    <p>You</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <p>Me</p>
                </li>
                <li>
                    <p>The Academy</p>
                </li>
            </ul>
        </p>
        <p>
            And to everyone else who inspired me.
        </p>
    </acknowledgement>
</FRAGMENT>
```
