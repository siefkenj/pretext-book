# `<creator>`

The `<creator>` tag credits the person or people associated with a
result, definition, or other statement. It is commonly used inside
[`<theorem>`](./theorem.mdx)-like elements to name the originator of the
result.

Use `<creator>` for short attributions such as individual names or a
small list of names.
