# `<attribution>`

The `<attribution>` element gives the source, speaker, or credit line for
a quotation or similar borrowed material. It most commonly follows a
[`<blockquote>`](./blockquote.mdx).

## Syntax

<SyntaxDisplay name="attribution" />

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="section subsection">
    <blockquote>
        <p>Pure mathematics is, in its way, the poetry of logical ideas.</p>
        <attribution>Albert Einstein</attribution>
    </blockquote>
</FRAGMENT>
```
