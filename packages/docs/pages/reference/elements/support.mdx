# `<support>`

The `<support>` element records a funding or support statement in
[`<bibinfo>`](./bibinfo.mdx). It is commonly used for acknowledgments of
grants, sponsoring institutions, or other assistance connected to the
document.

Because `<support>` is metadata, it belongs with publication details
rather than in the main body text.

## Syntax

<SyntaxDisplay name="support" />

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="frontmatter bibinfo">
    <support>
        <p>
            This work was supported in part by a departmental teaching
            innovation grant.
        </p>
    </support>
</FRAGMENT>
```

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="frontmatter bibinfo">
    <support>
        <p>
            We thank student testers and volunteer contributors for
            their assistance.
        </p>
    </support>
</FRAGMENT>
```
