# `<part>`

The `<part>` tag is a major division of a [`<book>`](./book.mdx). A part
groups multiple [`<chapter>`](./chapter.mdx) elements under a shared
title, often to organize a long work into larger thematic units.

Parts can also affect numbering. With structural parts, chapter
numbering restarts within each part; with decorative parts, the part is
presentational only.

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="pretext">
    <book>
        <title>Discrete Mathematics</title>
        <part>
            <title>Counting</title>
            <chapter>
                <title>Basic Principles</title>
                <p>This chapter introduces counting techniques.</p>
            </chapter>
        </part>
    </book>
</FRAGMENT>
```
