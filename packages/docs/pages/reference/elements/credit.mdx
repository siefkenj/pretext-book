# `<credit>`

The `<credit>` tag records a special credit in
[`<bibinfo>`](./bibinfo.mdx), such as a related work with its own author
or a production role like cover design. These credits are then available
for the generated [`<titlepage>`](./titlepage.mdx) and
[`<colophon>`](./colophon.mdx).

Depending on the kind of credit, use either a [`<title>`](./title.mdx)
with one or more [`<author>`](./author.mdx) elements, or a
[`<role>`](./role.mdx) with an [`<entity>`](./entity.mdx).

<SyntaxDisplay name="credit" />

## Examples

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="frontmatter bibinfo">
    <credit>
        <title>Companion workbook</title>
        <author>
            <personname>Riley Chen</personname>
        </author>
    </credit>
</FRAGMENT>
```

```ptx-example
<FRAGMENT parents="frontmatter bibinfo">
    <credit>
        <role>Cover design</role>
        <entity>Studio North</entity>
    </credit>
</FRAGMENT>
```
